# Product / Delivery Guide (Workflow + MVP + Backlog)

## Your job in discovery
- Translate outcomes into workflows (story map or use-case slices).
- Drive MVP slicing and scope boundaries (in/out).
- Ensure acceptance criteria exists for MVP-critical items.
- Produce a delivery-ready handover packet and backlog.

## Outputs you own
- Workflow model and MVP slices.
- Prioritized backlog with acceptance criteria.
- Refinement cadence and decision log.

## Watch-outs
- A feature list is not a workflow.
- If the PO can’t commit to tradeoffs, your backlog will be unstable.
