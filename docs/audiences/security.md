# Security / GRC Guide (Questionnaires + Evidence Pack)

## Your job in discovery
- Start early to avoid late-stage deal blockers.
- Request the customer questionnaire and required evidence list.
- Map questions to a single evidence pack, assign owners, track status.

## Outputs you own
- Evidence pack index and links.
- Questionnaire mapping table (domain → evidence → owner → status).
- Blockers list with dates and actions.

## Partnership with SA
Most questionnaire answers require architecture and operational details: coordinate closely with the Solutions Architect.
