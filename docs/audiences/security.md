# Security / Compliance / GRC Guide

## Why your role matters
- You prevent security and procurement from appearing only after technical and commercial decisions have already been made.
- You create one evidence path instead of answering every questionnaire from scratch.
- You make blockers visible early enough to affect scope, timing, or packaging decisions.

## What you own
- Start early to avoid late-stage deal blockers.
- Request the customer questionnaire and required evidence list.
- Map questions to a single evidence pack, assign owners, track status.

## Decisions you should make
- Which evidence is required now versus later.
- Which questions need SA input, legal input, or escalation.
- Whether a security blocker changes the discovery plan or PoV scope.

## Where you are needed by phase

| Phase | Your involvement | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| 0. Intake | Support | Flags whether a security or procurement gate exists |
| 1. Sales discovery | Optional | Helps surface onboarding requirements early |
| 2. Workflow workshop | Limited | Join only when workflows involve regulated actions or controls |
| 3. Technical deep dive | Support | Aligns technical constraints with likely questionnaire topics |
| 4. Security / procurement | Lead | This is the main session for your role |
| 5. Prototype / PoV | Support | Ensures the PoV does not violate customer data or identity constraints |
| 6. Handover | Review | Confirms remaining blockers and evidence gaps are visible to delivery |

## What to request early
- The questionnaire or onboarding checklist.
- Required certifications, testing reports, or policy evidence.
- Data residency and regulatory constraints.
- The customer timeline for vendor review and approval.

## Outputs you own
- Evidence pack index and links.
- Questionnaire mapping table (domain → evidence → owner → status).
- Blockers list with dates and actions.

## What good looks like
- The same evidence can answer most recurring questions.
- Open gaps are tracked with owners and dates.
- Security constraints are clear before custom build work starts.

## Partnership with SA
Most questionnaire answers require architecture and operational details: coordinate closely with the Solutions Architect.
